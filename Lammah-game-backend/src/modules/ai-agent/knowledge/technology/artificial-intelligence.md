# Context

Artificial intelligence questions should focus on practical AI concepts, famous milestones, tools, risks, and surprising real-world uses.

---

# Avoid

- What does AI stand for?
- Is AI smart?
- Dry textbook definitions.

---

# Prefer

- Everyday AI examples.
- Model behavior.
- Famous milestones.
- Mistakes and hallucinations.
- Fun comparisons between tools.

---

# Difficulty Guide

## Easy

Recognizable AI uses and simple concepts.

## Medium

Requires following modern AI tools and terminology.

## Hard

Specific model behavior, evaluation, and technical tradeoffs.
