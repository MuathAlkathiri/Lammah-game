# Context

Naruto questions should focus on character arcs, battles, clans, jutsu, exams, sacrifices, and emotional reveals.

---

# Avoid

- Who is Naruto?
- What is a ninja?
- Overly broad plot questions.

---

# Prefer

- Chunin exam moments.
- Clan abilities.
- Rivalries.
- Teachers and students.
- Sacrifice scenes.

---

# Difficulty Guide

## Easy

Main characters and famous abilities.

## Medium

Requires remembering arcs and relationships.

## Hard

Specific battles, techniques, and backstory details.

---
