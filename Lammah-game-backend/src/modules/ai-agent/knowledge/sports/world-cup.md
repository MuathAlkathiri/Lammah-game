# Context

World Cup questions should focus on memorable football moments, finals, penalties, iconic players, famous saves, shocking exits, and host-country stories.
The tone should feel like friends testing each other's football memory, not a school exam.

---

# Avoid

- Who won World Cup 2022?
- Where was World Cup 2022 held?
- Number of football players.
- Basic tournament definitions.
- Repeating the same final more than once in a batch.

---

# Prefer

- Historical moments.
- Finals.
- Records.
- Famous incidents.
- Player achievements.
- Goalkeeper moments.
- Underdog stories.
- Host nations with unusual stories.

---

# Difficulty Guide

## Easy

Use famous moments that most football fans remember, but avoid direct winner/host questions.

## Medium

Requires following World Cup history beyond the latest tournament.

## Hard

Use older tournaments, unusual records, or specific match incidents that serious fans know.

---

# Good Question Examples

- أي منتخب خسر نهائي كأس العالم 2014 بعد هدف ماريو غوتزه في الأشواط الإضافية؟
- من هو الحارس الذي اشتهر برقصة التصدي في نهائي كأس العالم 2022؟
- أي دولة استضافت كأس العالم مرتين قبل أن تفوز باللقب لأول مرة؟

---

# Bad Question Examples

- من فاز بكأس العالم 2022؟
- أين أقيم كأس العالم 2022؟
- كم عدد اللاعبين في كرة القدم؟
