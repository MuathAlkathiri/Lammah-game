# Context

Champions League questions should focus on dramatic nights, comebacks, finals, iconic goals, managers, rivalries, and records.

---

# Avoid

- Who won the latest Champions League?
- What does UEFA stand for?
- How many players are on a football team?

---

# Prefer

- Comebacks.
- Final goals.
- Legendary teams.
- Famous manager decisions.
- Records with a story.

---

# Difficulty Guide

## Easy

Famous clubs, finals, and widely remembered moments.

## Medium

Requires regular football following.

## Hard

Specific seasons, scorers, or tactical moments.

---

# Good Question Examples

- أي فريق قلب خسارته الثقيلة أمام باريس في ريمونتادا شهيرة عام 2017؟

---

# Bad Question Examples

- من فاز بدوري الأبطال العام الماضي؟
